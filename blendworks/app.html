How to Use BlendWorks

1. Add Your Lists

Click "Add List" and enter items you want to combine. Each item should be on a new line.

Example:
Colors: Red, Blue, Green
Sizes: Small, Medium, Large

2. Configure Settings

Adjust separator, duplicates handling, and other options in the sidebar.

3. Generate & Export

Click "Generate Combinations" and export your results in various formats.
